Arctic Monkeys

English rock band

Arctic Monkeys are an English rock band formed in Sheffield in 2002. The band consists of lead vocalist and rhythm guitarist Alex Turner, lead guitarist Jamie Cook, bassist Nick O'Malley, and drummer Matt Helders. They first gained recognition in the mid-2000s with their debut album "Whatever People Say I Am, That's What I'm Not," which became the fastest-selling debut album in UK chart history at the time.
